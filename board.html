The COPD Foundation



I met Jean Wright, the CEO of The COPD Foundation many years back when she was the Chief Innovation Officer of Atrium Health, one of the largest healthcare systems in North Carolina. We shared a passion for innovation and the notion of what’s possible. When Jean became the CEO of The COPD Foundation, she called to ask if I could help with strategy and scaling the organization. Of course, it was a resounding “yes”. We have set bold goals to eradicate COPD and galvanize a community of 10 million. And we are just getting started.

Renaissance West Community Initiative



I have been learning Spanish for 20 years and am still waiting for the day to be fluent! I love my Spanish professors and would love to do what they do. I mentioned this to my sister, and she said, “Why don’t you teach one English as a 2nd language class?” I joined CPCC and was placed at Renaissance West Community Initiative to teach 4 hours a week. I wanted to learn more about RWCI, so I set up an appointment with Mack McDonald, the CEO. Within minutes we started talking about strategy. An hour later, he asked if I would consider being on the board. It was a great honor. RWCI is dedicated to breaking intergenerational poverty by building a purpose driven community. Mack and the Board are nothing short of spectacular. As one of the few entrepreneurs, I offer a different perspective and I learn from the amazing professionals from large corporations and government positions. I am excited to do great things together.


Renaissance West Community Initiative


https://www.rwci.org/
