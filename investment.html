Investment Spotlight

Dr. Carreon and Acacia

“As a first-time CEO, Cindy has helped me and our executive team scale our company. Her drive is challenging, her encouragement is uplifting, and her advice has been critical.”

  1. A Big Need in Mental Health

    For many, depression medication proves effective. However, for 10-30% of individuals, their medications eventually become ineffective, resulting in treatment-resistant depression. This is the problem Dr. Carreon is addressing.

  2. Innovative Treatment from The Stanford Brain Stimulation Lab

    Dr. Carreon, a Stanford-trained psychiatrist, found hope for treatment-resistant depression through innovative research at the Stanford Brain Stimulation Lab. He pioneered Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS), a non-invasive treatment, reducing the standard six-week therapy to just five days.

  3. Winning the fight against depression

    Based on this research and Dr. Carreon's unwavering commitment, he founded the Acacia Research Center along with the Acacia Clinic, which has conducted more five-day accelerated treatments than any other organization in the world.

  4. Acacia has a 77% success rate of reducing depression.

    Our Involvement We roll up our sleeves (one of our core values) to actively support our portfolio companies. In the case of Acacia, we engage in weekly meetings with the team to discuss go-to-market strategies and growth partnerships, as well as participate in quarterly board meetings.
