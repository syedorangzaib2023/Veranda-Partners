3 Words Changed Everything

and that is why the
Social Impact Fund was birthed

Do Your Best

These are the words my father shared with me when I asked him what I would do without him. He was more than a father; he was my best friend, my unwavering cheerleader, and my rock. Every day, every moment, I live by those words.

A Heart of Compassion:
Welcoming the Pharmacist

Only my Dad would know that his local pharmacist had nowhere to go on Thanksgiving. When my cousin arrived at my parents house for Thanksgiving dinner, she asked, “Who is that?” I said, “that is my father’s pharmacist”. Without a pause she laughed and said, “of course it is!” The compassion that my Dad extended to those around him knew no bounds, and his actions spoke louder than words.

A Legacy of Kindness and BrowniesWe invest in just a select few companies.

Not a day went by that my Mom did not make someone’s day brighter. One of her many acts of love was baking the most delicious brownies. Even if we weren't home, Mom's brownies greeted anyone who walked through our door. In her honor, we are committed to doing an act of kindness every day.
